IdeaCrew President **Trevor Garner** announced he is passing the chair role of the Ulman Foundation Board of Directors at the close of what was described on stage as a record-setting Blue Jeans & Bow Ties Ball.

During his remarks, Garner thanked attendees for their support and pointed to the momentum around Ulman Foundation's mission. He then introduced **Liz O'Conor** as the incoming chair, describing her as a longtime board member and dedicated champion for young adults.

Garner also shared a fundraising update from the event, telling the audience the gala had surpassed the **$1 million** mark and was conservatively estimated at approximately **$1.15 million** by the end of the evening.

The leadership transition reflects a continued focus on community impact and long-term stewardship, with O'Conor set to lead the board into its next chapter.
