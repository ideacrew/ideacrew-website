IdeaCrew's Manager of Security Operations, **Desire Banse**, recently joined CyberSymposiums as a featured panelist for a discussion on the future of modern Security Operations Centers (SOCs).

CyberSymposiums is a cybersecurity event platform that brings together security leaders, practitioners, and technology experts for focused panels on emerging threats, practical operations challenges, and AI-driven security innovation.

Banse appeared on the panel titled **"Revolutionizing SOC Operations: Advanced AI Reasoning and the Future of Level 2/3 Security Innovations,"** alongside:

- Liam Barnes, Head of Marketing, Legion Security
- Christopher Crowley, Independent Consultant, Montance
- Jamie Morrison, Director of Security Operations Center, One Source

The panel explored how AI can strengthen threat detection and response, how organizations can overcome implementation hurdles, and how leaders can mentor the next generation of security analysts while keeping human judgment at the center of SOC operations.
