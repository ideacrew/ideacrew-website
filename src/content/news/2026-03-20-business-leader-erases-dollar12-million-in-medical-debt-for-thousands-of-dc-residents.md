Trevor Garner, a technology and financial services executive with over 31 years of experience, is making a profound impact on the Washington, D.C. community. As a C-suite leader at **IdeaCrew**, Garner and his colleagues took a remarkable step in giving back — purchasing **$12 million worth of medical debt** and erasing it for more than **12,000 D.C. residents**.

For many of those affected, the burden of unresolved medical debt had far-reaching consequences, damaging credit scores and blocking access to essential needs like housing and transportation.

_"These are folks that probably cannot resolve this debt on their own, and that creates more problems, including impacting their credit. People can't buy a car or qualify for housing when their credit is pulled, so we are trying to help remove that burden."_

— Trevor Garner

Driven by the values instilled by his late father, Garner has made community citizenship a cornerstone of his leadership philosophy. His work at IdeaCrew reflects a growing movement of business leaders using their resources to create meaningful, lasting change in the communities they serve.
