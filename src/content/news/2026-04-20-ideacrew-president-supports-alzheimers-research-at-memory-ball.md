IdeaCrew President **Trevor Garner** joined colleague **Anna Thomas** on the dance floor at the 2026 Memory Ball Dancing Stars event, helping spotlight the fight against Alzheimer's disease and the families navigating it every day.

Hosted by the Alzheimer's Association Greater Maryland Chapter, the annual Memory Ball brings together community leaders to raise funds for research, care, and support services. According to CBS Baltimore's event coverage, the 2026 gala raised approximately **$1.2 million** through the Dancing Stars effort.

Garner and Thomas danced in honor of Beth Thomas and shared their story as part of the broader campaign to increase awareness for Alzheimer's and related dementias. Their participation reflects IdeaCrew's continued commitment to service-oriented leadership and community impact.

The Alzheimer's Association Memory Ball page also reported campaign totals near **$1.09 million raised toward a $1.25 million goal** at the time of posting, with Dancing Stars competing for votes to support the cause.
